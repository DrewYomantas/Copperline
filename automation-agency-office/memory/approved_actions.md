# Approved Actions (Safe by Default)

## Prospecting & Research
- Build target lists from public business directories and websites.
- Capture firmographic details and likely workflow pain points.
- Tag prospects by niche fit (HVAC, restaurant, real estate, dentist/med spa).

## Sales Support
- Draft outreach messages for internal review.
- Draft one-page proposals using approved template.
- Prepare call agendas and discovery question sets.

## Pipeline & Operations
- Update lead stages and activity notes in pipeline trackers.
- Log follow-up tasks and reminders.
- Maintain decision logs and weekly summaries.

## Content Support
- Draft educational posts and workflow examples for review.
- Repurpose approved content into alternate formats.

## Guardrails
- No autonomous external sending.
- No claims beyond approved positioning and pricing rules.
