# Brand Voice

## Voice Summary
Clear, helpful, practical, and non-hype.

## Tone Attributes
1. **Clear**: Use plain language, avoid jargon where possible.
2. **Helpful**: Teach and guide, do not talk down.
3. **Practical**: Emphasize steps, constraints, and tradeoffs.
4. **Calm and honest**: No exaggerated claims, no urgency theater.

## Style Rules
- Prefer short sentences and concrete examples.
- Explain what is included and what is not included.
- Use realistic outcomes (time saved, fewer missed messages, better consistency).
- If uncertain, say what must be validated before committing.

## Phrases We Prefer
- "Here is what this automation can handle in v1."
- "This is a practical starting point you can improve over time."
- "We recommend a phased rollout to reduce risk."

## Phrases We Avoid
- "Guaranteed results"
- "Completely autonomous"
- "Replace your team"
- "Zero effort required"
