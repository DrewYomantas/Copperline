# Content Strategy (Simple, Practical)

## Objective
Build trust with local businesses by demonstrating practical automation examples and business efficiency tips.

## Core Content Pillars
1. **Automation Examples**
   - Before/after workflow snapshots
   - Simple use-case demos for each v1 service
2. **Efficiency Tips**
   - Process cleanup tips before automation
   - Inbox handling and follow-up consistency tactics
3. **Implementation Reality**
   - What v1 can and cannot do
   - Common rollout mistakes and how to avoid them

## Channel Focus (v1)
- LinkedIn (primary)
- Short-form video or reels (optional secondary)

## Weekly Cadence
- 2 educational posts (tips/checklists)
- 1 practical example post (mini case workflow, anonymized)
- 1 credibility post (process walkthrough, FAQ, or lesson learned)

## Content Rules
- No fabricated testimonials or proof claims.
- No guaranteed revenue claims.
- Keep language plain and specific.
- Include clear call-to-action (e.g., "reply for workflow checklist").
