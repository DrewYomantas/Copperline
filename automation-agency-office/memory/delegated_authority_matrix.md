# Delegated Authority Matrix

## Level 1 — Autonomous Internal Tasks
**Definition:** Can be completed without founder involvement.

Examples:
- Internal documentation updates
- Public prospect research
- Drafting (outreach, proposals, content)
- Pipeline status updates

---

## Level 2 — Founder Review Required
**Definition:** Work can be prepared by agent but must be reviewed before use.

Examples:
- Outreach copy for specific prospects
- Proposal drafts with pricing in-range
- Public-facing content drafts
- Process recommendations that affect team workflows

---

## Level 3 — Founder Approval Required
**Definition:** Cannot proceed without explicit founder approval.

Examples:
- Sending any external communication
- Publishing public claims or case references
- Quotes outside approved price bands
- Contractual commitments or scope exceptions
- Accessing/changing sensitive tools, credentials, or billing systems
