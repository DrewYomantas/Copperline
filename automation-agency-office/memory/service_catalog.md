# Service Catalog (v1)

## 1) FAQ Chatbot
### Problem
Teams repeatedly answer the same basic customer questions via website chat, social DMs, or SMS.

### Deliverables
- FAQ intent list and answer library (client-approved)
- Chatbot flow for top recurring questions
- Escalation path to human for unknown/complex cases
- Basic reporting on common inquiries and fallback rate

### Timeline
- Typical setup: **5-10 business days**

### Price Band
- **$500-$1,500** depending on channels, FAQ volume, and escalation logic.

---

## 2) Appointment / Booking Automation
### Problem
Manual booking coordination causes delays, double-bookings, and after-hours response gaps.

### Deliverables
- Intake flow for booking requests
- Calendar integration and confirmation workflow
- Reminder messages (where supported)
- Basic reschedule/cancel path with handoff for exceptions

### Timeline
- Typical setup: **7-14 business days**

### Price Band
- **$800-$2,000** depending on scheduling rules and integrations.

---

## 3) Inbox / Form Triage Automation
### Problem
Incoming emails/forms are manually sorted, causing slow response times and missed requests.

### Deliverables
- Routing rules by inquiry type/priority
- Tagging/categorization logic
- Assignment notifications to correct team member
- Exception queue for uncertain cases

### Timeline
- Typical setup: **5-12 business days**

### Price Band
- **$700-$1,800** based on volume, rules complexity, and systems connected.

---

## 4) Lead Capture + Follow-up Automation
### Problem
Leads come in from multiple sources, and follow-up is inconsistent or delayed.

### Deliverables
- Lead intake capture from selected channels/forms
- Standardized lead record creation
- Initial follow-up sequence (email/SMS where compliant)
- Simple pipeline stage updates and alerts for manual handoff

### Timeline
- Typical setup: **7-14 business days**

### Price Band
- **$900-$2,500** depending on channel count and follow-up logic depth.

## Notes
- Maintenance/support can be quoted separately as monthly retainers.
- Custom work outside v1 scope requires written change request.
